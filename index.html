I’m Suryam Gupta & I'm a Data Scientist aspirant

I am currently a Master's student in Data Analytics and Computational Social Science (DACSS) at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am intrigued by Data Science and the concept of finding meaningful insights and underlying patterns from data and building predictive models.
